13. tolong di jawab soalnya tugasnya di periksa


PENGGUNAAN SHOULD DO, SHOULD BE DOING, SHOULD HAVE DONE AND SHOULDN'T HAVE DONEJawaban :Pendahuluan

Should merupakan salah satu bentuk modal verb. Sebelum kita memberikan saran dalam setiap situasi yang diberikan, kita perlu memahami kapan menggunakan SHOULD DO, SHOULD BE DOING, SHOULD HAVE DONE dan SHOULDN'T HAVE DONE.

SHOULD + INFINITE (Verb 1)

Digunakan untuk memberikan saran dan rekomendasi, contoh :

1. Your father is waiting in the car park, you should go now.

2. Anna's bedroom is very messy, she should tidy up her bedroom now.


SHOULD + BE ING

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan bahwa subyek dalam kalimat tidak memenuhi kewajibannya (obligation) dan bertindak tidak bertanggung jawab.

1. You should be wearing you seatbelt. (The person isn't wearing one right now)

2. We should be studying for the test. (We aren't studying and we should)


SHOULD + HAVE + PAST PARTICIPLE

Digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang diharapkan terjadi di masa lalu tetapi tidak terjadi atau memberikan saran tentang masa lalu atau menyatakan penyesalan.

1. You should have given the report to your boss yesterday when he asked for it.

2. I should have studied harder!


SHOULDN'T + HAVE + PAST PAST PARTICIPLE

Digunakan untuk menyatakan  bahwa sesuatu bukanlah ide yang bagus, tetapi tetap dilakukan.

1. I got wet yesterday, I shouldn't have left my umbrella at home.

2. I shouldn't eaten so much cake.

Pembahasan

Situation 1

Setiawan and his wife, Peni, has good jobs as a professional in Bandung CIty. Setiawan was offered a high paying job in Jakarta, which he immediately accepted. Peni was shocked when he came home that evening and told her the news. She liked her job and the people she worked with. She didn't want to move away and look for another job.

1.  Setiawan  should have discussed with Peni before make a decision.

2. Setiawan shouldn't have decided without asking Peni's opinion.

3. Setiawan should think carefully before making a decision next time.

Situation 2

For four years, Mahadewi had been saving her money for a trip to Bali. Her brother, Prabu, had a good job, but spent all of his money on expensive cars, clothes and entertainment. Suddenly, Prabu was fired from work and had no money to support himself while he looked for another job.  Mahadewi lent him nearly of all her saving, and within three weeks, he spent it all on his cars, clothes and expensive restaurants.

1. Mahadewi shouldn't have lent all her money to Prabu.

2. Mahadewi should have thought very carefully before she lent the money to Prabu.

3. Prabu shouldn't have spent all the money on cars, clothes and expensive restaurants.

4. Prabu should learn to manage his money wisely.


Situation 3

Wahyudi didn't study for the test.  During the exam, he panicked and started looking at other students' paper. He didn't think the teacher saw him, but the teacher did. The teacher warned him once to stop cheating, but he continued. As a result, the teacher took Wahyudi's test paper, told him to leave the room and failed him on exam.

1.  Wahyudi should have studied for the test.

2. Wahyudi should have stopped cheating when the teacher warned him.

3. Wahyudi should study hard next time.
