Induksi MateMAtik
p(k) +n(k+1) = p(k+1)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

induksi

1) 2 + 4 + 6 + . . . + 2n =  n(n+1)

p(n) = n(n+1)
p(k) =  k(k+1)
n(k+1) =  2(k+1)
p(k+1) =  (k+1)(k+1+1) = (k+1)(k+2)

p(k) +n(k+1) = p(k+1)

k(k+1) + 2(k+1) =  (k+1)(k+2)
(k+1)(k+2) = (k+1)(k+2)
ruas kiri = ruas kanan (terbukti)

__

2) 1 + 2 + 3 + . . . + n =  1/2 n (n+1)

[tex]\sf p(n) = \frac{1}{2}n(n+1)[/tex]

[tex]\sf p(k) = \frac{1}{2}k(k+1)[/tex]

n(k+1) = k+1

[tex]\sf p(k+1) = \frac{1}{2}(k+1)(k+1+1) = \frac{1}{2}(k+1)(k+2)[/tex]

p(k) +n(k+1) = p(k+1)
