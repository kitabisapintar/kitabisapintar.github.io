Berikut pilihan yang benar untuk soal pilihan ganda LKS bahasa inggris kelas 9 halaman 11 semester satu,  nomor 7 sampai 15 yaitu:

A. good luck.A. im happy for you.A. im sure you will get a very big applause.D. Thank you, I hope so.C. How nice of you to say so!B. that greats.B. congratulations Siti.C. Ester the best singer.D. congratulating.Pembahasan

Expressing congratulations ialah memberi selamat kepada orang lain dalam bahasa Inggris dapat dilakukan dengan mengucapkan "Congratulations!" Congratulations is an expression used to convey joy, admiration, and praise to someone in recognition of their achievements, success, or good fortune. It is a way to show happiness and encouragement towards someone's accomplishments or positive events in their life. By congratulating someone, you acknowledge their efforts and let them know that their achievements are noteworthy and appreciated.

Berikut ini adalah beberapa contoh kalimat untuk memberi selamat kepada orang lain dalam bahasa Inggris:

Congratulations on your promotion! You truly deserve it.I wanted to congratulate you on your graduation. Well done!Congratulations on your wedding! Wishing you a lifetime of love and happiness.I'm so proud of you for winning the competition. Congratulations!Congratulations on your new job. I know you'll excel in your new role.I wanted to send my heartfelt congratulations on the arrival of your beautiful baby girl/boy.Congratulations on your well-deserved scholarship. Your hard work paid off!Wishing you a happy anniversary! Congratulations on reaching this milestone together.Congratulations on completing your degree.
