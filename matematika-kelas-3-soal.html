= 1/2 jarak C ke GS

= 1/2 × SC × CG / GS

= 1/2 × 1/2 r√2 × r / (1/2 r√6)

= 1/6 r√3

= √3 satuan

O tengah ABCD

tinggi limas

= TO

= √(TA² - AO²)

= √(6² - (1/2 . 4√2)²)

= √(36 - 8)

= 2√7

R tengah TO

TP/TA = TR/TO = 3/6 = 1/2

TR = 1/2 TO

TR = OR

= 1/2 TO

= 1/2 × 2√7
