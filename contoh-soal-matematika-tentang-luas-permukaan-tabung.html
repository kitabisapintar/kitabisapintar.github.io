Luas Permukaan Tabung.Lp = 2 × Luas Alas + Luas Selimut TabungLp [tex]= (2 \times \pi \times {r}^{2}) + (2 \times \pi \times r \times t)[/tex]Lp [tex]= (2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times {7}^{2}) \: {cm}^{2} + (2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 7 \times 10) \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= (2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 49) \: {cm}^{2} + (2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 70) \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= (2 \times 22 \times 7) \: {cm}^{2} + (2 \times 22 \times 10) \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 308 \: {cm}^{2} + 440 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 748 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]

Luas Permukaan ½ Bola.Lp = ½ × Luas Permukaan BolaLp [tex]= \frac{1}{2} \times (4 \times \pi \times {r}^{2})[/tex]Lp [tex]= 2 \times \pi \times {r}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times {(7)}^{2} \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 49 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 2 \times 22 \times 7 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]Lp [tex]= 308 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]

Volume Tabung.V = Luas Alas × Tinggi TabungV [tex]= (\pi \times {r}^{2}) \times t[/tex]V [tex]= (\frac{22}{7} \times {7}^{2}) \: {cm}^{2} \times 10 \: cm[/tex]V [tex]= (\frac{22}{7} \times 49) \: {cm}^{2} \times 10 \: cm[/tex]V [tex]= 154 \: {cm}^{2} \times 10 \: cm[/tex]V [tex]= 1.540 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Volume ½ Bola.V = ½ × Volume BolaV [tex]= \frac{1}{2} \times (\frac{4}{3} \times \pi \times {r}^{3})[/tex]V [tex]= \frac{2}{3} \times \pi \times {r}^{3} [/tex]V [tex]= \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{22}{7} \times {7}^{3} \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]V [tex]= \frac{44}{21} \times 343 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]V [tex]= 718,67 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Luas Permukaan Gabungan.L = Luas Permukaan Tabung + Luas Permukaan ½ BolaL [tex]= 748 \: {cm}^{2} + 308 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]L [tex]= 1.056 \: {cm}^{2} [/tex]

Volume Gabungan.V = Volume Tabung + Volume ½ BolaV [tex]= 1.540 \: {cm}^{3} + 718,67 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]V [tex]= 2.258,67 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]
